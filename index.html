Our Heritage

Ikuu Boys High School was established in 1964 as a provincial day secondary school under the sponsorship of the PCEA. The school relocated to its current site in 1965 through land donated by the local community. Our mission is to provide quality secondary education and promote holistic development, fostering academic and moral excellence.

Boarding & Discipline

Discipline is a cornerstone of the boarding system. The structured environment allows students to manage time effectively, balancing academic work and rest, while hostel life promotes teamwork and tolerance.

Languages Department

Transitioning to the CBC by 2027, the department is evolving into a "Linguistic Centre". We promote critical thinking and ethical reasoning through deep literary analysis and creativity.

Leadership Philosophy

Education at Ikuu is strategic, like a game of chess. The "Maroon Spirit" defines our brotherhood, where student leaders are encouraged to nurture renewable energy solutions and refuse to settle for mediocrity.
