Institutional Profile

Ikuu Boys High School was established in 1964 as a provincial day secondary school under the sponsorship of the Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA). The school moved to its current location in 1965, made possible by land donated by the local community.

Our Mission

To provide quality secondary education and promote holistic development, fostering academic and moral excellence.
