Our Heritage

Over 60 Years of Institutional Excellence

Ikuu Boys' High School was established in 1964 as a provincial day secondary school on the grounds currently occupied by Ikuu Girls' High School, under the sponsorship of the **Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA)**.

In 1965, the school relocated to its current site through generous land donations and infrastructural support from the local community. Through decades of steady growth, development, and impeccable quality standards, the school officially transitioned into a fully fledged National School in 2012, admitting its first national cohort in 2013.
