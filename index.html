Our Heritage

Ikuu Boys High School was established in 1964 as a provincial day secondary school under the sponsorship of the PCEA. The school relocated to its current site in 1965 through land donated by the local community.

Boarding & Discipline

Discipline is the cornerstone of our system. Our structured environment allows students to manage time effectively, balancing academic rigor with personal growth in our 10 modern hostels.

Languages Department

Transitioning to the CBC, we are evolving into a "Linguistic Centre" that promotes critical thinking, empathy, and expressive confidence.
