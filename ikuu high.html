Our Heritage

Founded in 1964 as a provincial day secondary school under the historic sponsorship of the Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA), Ikuu Boys originally sat on grounds now housing Ikuu Girls. In 1965, thanks to generous land and development resources donated by the passionate local community, the school moved to its current location.

Through structural growth and progressive curriculum expansions—including launching 'A' Levels in 1981 and transitioning to provincial boarding in 2006—the institution reached its crowning milestone in 2012, officially earning its elevation to a fully-fledged National School status.
